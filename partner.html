Partner With Us

No single organization will solve AI alignment. The problem is too vast.

Are you a philanthropist? Consider co-funding this critical work.

We mine brain science for safety insights. We build interpretability tools that actually work. And our bets seem to be paying off.

But for every breakthrough we fund, three more sit on the shelf. The landscape of solutions is vast, and our resources are limited. That's where you can help.

Choose how you lead

Direct Action

Co-fund directly through our 501(c)(3). It is simple and tax-advantaged. We handle the vetting, the administration, and the reporting. You gain leverage on your giving without the operational labor.

Strategic Support

Use your own foundation or family office. We provide the due diligence, strategic advising, and monitoring. You maintain full control while benefiting from our deep technical intelligence.

Field Growth

Support the ecosystem. Help fund the coalitions, policies, and partnerships that move the entire field forward. Sometimes the highest-leverage move is building the bridge for others to follow.

When we pool capital, we spread risk. We ensure no promising approach dies simply because it fell outside one funder's priorities. Collaboration is how we survive uncertainty.
