Who We Are

The Alignment Foundation supports research to ensure humans remain in control of their AI-powered future. We power the bold, underfunded bleeding-edge AI alignment research that will help humanity shape its own destiny.

We are grateful to our founding funders, AE Studio and the Stern family.
